Common Exercises

Everyone is expected to do some forms of excercises throughout the day to keep the heart pumping and we encourage our clients to take up swimming as that is indeed very benefical for the heart and is fun in general

Burpee
Burpees are a great full body excercise to increase your strength and endurance.Begin in a standing position and then drop into a squat and extent your hands forward and kick your feet back and then forward again quicly and then jump up form a squatted position
Planks
Planks help build up your inner core which is what a lot of people usually struggle.In order to perform a plank get on the ground rest your elbows out and feet extended and hold your inner core in that position
Mountain Climber
Mountain climbers are a good cardio set. Place your hands on your mat flat and then bring each leg in at a time and and repeat a couple of times.
