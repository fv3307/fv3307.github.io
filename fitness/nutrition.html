Meal of the Week

Herb Roasted Chicken

Ingredients :

  • bonelss, skinless chicken breasts ( oragnic preferred)
  • 1 tsp Herbes de Provence
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • ¼ tsp pepper

Instructions :

combine all the ingredients in a plastic bag and marinate for atleast half an hour and then preheat the over at 350 degrees and bake the chicken until the juices run clear
